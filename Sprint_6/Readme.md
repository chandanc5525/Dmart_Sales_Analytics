---

You are working as a Junior Data Analyst in the Business Intelligence Team at DMart.
```
The company has collected large sales transaction data from multiple:
- Regions
- Cities
- Product Categories
- Stores
```
---

```

- Region-wise revenue
- Product-wise sales
- High-performing categories
- Low-performing regions
- Profitability trends
```
---

Instead of analyzing raw transactional records, you are asked to create categorized and summarized business reports using Excel formulas and analytical tools.
